History of the Company

IT Solutions was established in 2001 as a result of the shared vision of Naufal Salim, Samael Smith, and Ramadhani Sampoerna to change the paradigm in the information technology industry. With strong backgrounds in technology and business, the trio came together to respond to the need for innovative and high-quality IT solutions. The company was born out of a desire to not only provide products and services, but also to be a leader in bringing deep digital transformation to clients from various industry sectors.

Company Values

At IT Solutions, we honor the values of boundless innovation, excellence in service, unwavering integrity, and inspiring collaboration. These values form the basis of our corporate culture that promotes creativity, integrity, and professional growth. We ensure that every step we take and every decision we make is aligned with these values to deliver the best results for our clients.

Our vision is to be a leader in digital transformation by providing leading-edge IT solutions that enable businesses to reach their full potential. Our mission is to empower clients with technology that solves problems, improves efficiency and drives sustainable growth.
